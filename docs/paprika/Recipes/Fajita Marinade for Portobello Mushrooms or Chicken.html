Fajita Marinade for Portobello Mushrooms or Chicken

★★★★★

Chicken, Gluten Free, Main Course, Mexican, Vegan

Ingredients

1 Tbsp balsamic vinegar

1 Tbsp water

3 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil

1 lime, juiced

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 tsp chili powder

1/2 tsp cumin

1/2 tsp salt


4-6 Portobello Mushroom caps or Chicken

Directions

Mix marinade ingredients
Put cleaned mushroom or chicken in ziploc bag and add marinade
Put in fridge for at least a few hours or overnight

Grill Chicken or Mushrooms, rest and slice

Serve with traditional fajita sides

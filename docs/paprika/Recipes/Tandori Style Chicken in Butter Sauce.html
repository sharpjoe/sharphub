Tandori Style Chicken in Butter Sauce

★★★★★

Chicken, Indian, Main Course, Protein

Difficulty : Easy Source : Linda Lawson / Nigella Lawson

Ingredients

For Tandori Chicken:


6 Butterflyed Boneless Thighs

3 TBSP Sharwood’s Tandoori Spice - Sharwoods Tandori Spice Mix

3 TBSP plain yogurt

3 TBSP corn oil

3 TBSP white wine vinegar

3 TBSP lemon juice


For Butter Sauce:

4 TBSP tomato puree

Water

1 inch cube of ginger, peeled, grated finely to a pulp

½ pint single (heavy) cream

1 tsp garam masala

¾ tsp salt

¼ tsp sugar

1 fresh hot green chili chopped fine

¼ tsp cayenne peper

1 TBSP fresh green coriander (cilantro) chopped fine

4 tsp lemon juice

1 tsp ground roasted cumin seeds

4 oz unsalted butter

Tandori style chicken – fresh cooked

Directions

Tandori Chicken

Mix all ingredients (except chicken) together. Pour over chicken and coat well

Cover and leave to marinate overnight in fridge. (I use a Ziploc bag)

Cook for about 25 minutes at 425.

Butter Sauce
Put the tomato paste in a clear measuring jug.
Add water slowly mixing as you go, to make up 8 fl oz of tomato sauce.
Add the ginger, cream, masala, salt, sugar, green chili, cayenne, coriander, lemon juice, and cumin.
Mix well.

Heat butter in sauté pan or large frying pan.
When butter has melted, add all ingredients in the measuring jug.
Bring to a simmer and cook on med heat for a minute mixing in butter as you do.
Add the chicken (drained) Stir once and put chicken piece on a warm serving platter.
Spoon sauce over top.
Serve over rice.

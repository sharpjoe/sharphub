Keevlings

Christmas, Cookies, Deserts

Difficulty : Easy Source : Mom

Ingredients

3 Vanilla Beans

½ LB unsalted butter

½ C sugar

1 C ground almond

1 ½ C Flour

½ LB powdered sugar (1 2/3rds cups)

Directions

1. Cream butter and sugar
2. Add ground almonds and then flour
3. Roll in crescent shape
4. Bake till slightly brown in 350* oven till slightly brown – About 10 min
5. Roll in powdered sugar & vanilla bean

Notes

3-3oz pkgs of slivered almonds = 2 cups ground
